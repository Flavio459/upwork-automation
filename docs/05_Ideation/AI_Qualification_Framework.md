> [!IMPORTANT]

## Accept

- client asks for audit, roadmap, architecture, or strategy
- workflow includes integrations, APIs, or multi-step operations
- opportunity implies a real business process, not a tiny one-off task
- the work can plausibly be executed with AI in 90%+ of the flow
- budget can support a consultative starting price

## Watch

- client wants automation but scope is still vague
- opportunity mentions AI but only as a buzzword
- the workflow is interesting, but the budget is still unclear
- the work may need a smaller entry offer before the full audit

## Reject

- simple task, low ticket, or commodity execution
- no real process to audit
- request is mostly manual support, admin, or data entry
- client expects implementation only, with no room for discovery
- AI cannot cover the majority of the flow safely

## Scoring Questions

1. Is there a business workflow, not just a task?
2. Can the value be expressed as risk reduction, speed, or leverage?
3. Is there enough complexity to justify an audit?
4. Can AI handle most of the execution with human oversight?
5. Can the ticket support a productized starting price?

## Decision Rule

- `Accept` if 4 or 5 answers are yes and the budget is plausible
- `Watch` if the opportunity is real but the framing needs work
- `Reject` if the opportunity is cheap, vague, or operationally trivial

## Next Step After `Accept`

1. registrar intake
2. validar viabilidade
3. montar costing e pricing
4. gerar proposta mestre em `pt-BR`
5. localizar para a lingua do cliente antes do envio
